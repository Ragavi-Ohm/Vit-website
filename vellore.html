VIT VELLORE

VIT Vellore Overview

VIT Vellore is one of the top-rated private engineering institutes. It has featured in the rank band of 1001-1200 in QS World University Ranking 2023. Admissions at VIT Vellore are currently open. VIT campus is spread across a vast area of 372 acres. The remarkable aspect of VIT is the state-of-the-art infrastructure provided for both students and faculty. Fully furnished and well-equipped labs decorate every building in the University. Moreover, the campus is fortified with ample features that support diverse events - Auditoriums, plush open spaces, outdoor stages and much more.A striking feature of VIT is the well-planned and comprehensive premises provided for both students and faculty. Centre for Technical Support (CTS) maintains the policies governing the use of VIT computing and IT communication resources. The IT Policy applies to the resources administered by the administrative departments such as Library, Computer Laboratories, Offices of the Institution, Hostels and Guest houses wherever the network facility was provided by the Institution. The smart classrooms are well designed to incorporate everything needed for a pleasant learning atmosphere. The wide spacious classrooms contain a white board and a projector that is used for teaching. As a result professors can switch to different modes of teaching as and when required so that the teaching isn’t monotonous and the lectures are put across in the best way possible. This hi-tech equipment enables the faculty to conduct classes in a way that enable students to make use of every available resource at the click of a button. These classrooms are also often used for seminars and events from time to time.

Placement

VIT Vellore placements 2022 commenced on July 15 for the 2022 graduating batch and witnessed participation of eight super dream companies -Microsoft, Dyte, Amazon, PayPal, DE Shaw India Pvt Ltd., Morgan Stanley, Udaan and WorkIndia. A total of 648 companies have visited the campus and 7,075 students have been placed as on Feb 4, 2022 during VIT Vellore placements 2022. Further, the highest package and average package offered during VIT Vellore placements 2022 were INR 75 LPA and INR 8.19 LPA, respectively. A humongous increase of70% was seen in the highest salary package offered so far for the 2022 graduating batch in comparison to that in 2021.

Fee structure

Vellore Institute of Technology offers 19 courses across 6 streams in which price range is approx ₹77,000 - ₹3.54 Lakhs (1st Year Fees).

Infrastructure

Spread across a vast area of372 acres, VIT Vellore has seven academic blocks in the Vellore campus, each specialising in its particular fields. One of the USPs of the Vellore campus is the ‘Anna Audi’ which is able to accommodate over1800+students. VIT Vellore provides 24 safe, comfortable, inclusive and secure accommodation for both boys and girls. Out of these, 18 hostels are for boys and six for girls. These hostels can accommodate over 22,000 students. Facilities such as AC and non-AC single and shared rooms, 24*7 water and power supply, in-house residential counselors, wardens, supervisors, mess, café, and round-the-day security among others are offered at the campus.
