VIT CHENNAI

VIT Chennai Overview

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) is a Deemed University located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu and was established in the year 1984. The Institute is accredited ‘Grade A’ by NAAC and is approved by University Grants Commission (UGC). VIT offers various Undergraduate, Integrated and Postgraduate courses in addition to PhD and M.Phil research programs.

Placement

VIT University Chennai has released the placement data wherein a total of 690 companies visited the University. They have 85-90% parcentage rate. The highest salary package offered was 45 LPA, and the average salary package offered is anywhere 6 LPA. Top recruiting companies are Microsoft, L&T, CTZ, Wipro, Goldman Sachs, Deloitt, PayPal, etc.

Fee structure

Vellore Institute of Technology offers 19 courses across 6 streams in which price range is approx ₹77,000 - ₹3.54 Lakhs (1st Year Fees).

Infrastructure

VIT University, Chennai campus spreads over 177 acres of land and provides various infrastructure and facilities for the students and faculty such as separate hostels for boys and girls, sports, auditorium and conference halls, library and gymnasium, etc. Other facilities like smart classrooms, internet, etc are also available.
