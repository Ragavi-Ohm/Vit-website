VIT BHOPAL

VIT Bhopal Overview

VIT Bhopal University is situated on 150 acres of sprawling lush green Campus at Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. It was established in the year 2017 and led by founder and Chancellor Dr. G Vishwanathan. VIT Bhopal is approved by University Grants Commission (UGC). VIT University Bhopal is currently accepting applications for its various undergraduate and postgraduate programmes for the academic year 2022-23. VIT Bhopal University offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various specializations. At the postgraduate level, it offers two-year full-time MCA program (Master of Computer Application) and M.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering. At the undergraduate level, it offers BBA, B.Com, B.Tech, and B.Sc. Computer Science (Specialization in Data Analytics).

Placement

  • VIT Bhopal has a dedicated Placement and Training centre for assisting the students to be well- versed in coding and aptitude skills for better performance in the job interviews. It helps the students to prepare for group discussions and be trained as per the current industry standards.
  • The highest package for VIT Bhopal University is 41.6 LPA. While the average package offered to students is 7.25 LPA. The highest placements were offered in the IT sector with 35% of the total placement share.
  • VIT Bhopal University has been consistently setting records of campus placements amongst private institutions in India. The institute has over 400 recruiters from Core Engineering and Software companies who visit the campus every year.

Infrastructure

  • The University library is centrally air-conditioned, well-protected with fire alarm, Flap Barrier, and CCTV surveillance. Along with the latest RFID technology, it features a specialized collection of books, national and international journals, and other resources in Sciences, Engineering and Technology, Biotechnology, Humanities, Social Sciences and Management ranging from printed books, e-books, back volumes and CDs\DVDs.
  • Each hostel room is well furnished.Provision stores are located in the hostel where students can buy essentials such as mattresses, pillows, buckets, mugs, locks, stationeries, books, soaps. Both AC and Non-AC rooms are available. All the hostels are wi-fi enabled. Mess facility is also available for the students. The hostels are compounded with the security detail and security cameras. The doctor is also available.
