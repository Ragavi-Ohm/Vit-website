VIT AMARAVATI

VIT Amaravati Overview

With a history of 37 years of innovation in educational and research domain, VIT has been a forerunner in delivering quality education. Consistently ranked among the top educational institutes in the country, the VIT group of institutions have had a proud tradition of pursuing knowledge and excellence. In keeping with this tradition, the leadership at VIT-AP resonates a dynamic blend of academic initiative and industry partnership with a vision of creating one of the finest academic destinations in the world. The VIT-AP campus, which is poised to become one of the country’s best campuses, offers several avenues to explore your interests, identify core competencies, and engage in an evolving lifecycle of education and growth. It is a place where students are offered immense opportunities to learn, practice, grow, and thrive and autonomous learning is encouraged in order to enable students to chart their own course.

Placement

  • The University has released its official placement statistics for the year 2022. The highest international package offered was INR 63 LPA. A total of 1,175 offers were made during VIT AP University placement 2022, out of which 246 were super dream offers and 354 were dream offers.
  • A rise of 26.13% was observed in the number of students placed as compared to 2021
  • A drop of 22.03% was observed in the number of companies visited as compared to 2021
  • An increase of 13.37% was observed in the average package as compared to 2021

Fee structure

Vellore Institute of Technology offers 19 courses across 6 streams in which price range is approx ₹77,000 - ₹3.54 Lakhs (1st Year Fees).

Infrastructure

  • The University library is centrally air-conditioned, well-protected with fire alarm, Flap Barrier, and CCTV surveillance. Along with the latest RFID technology, it features a specialized collection of books, national and international journals, and other resources in Sciences, Engineering and Technology, Biotechnology, Humanities, Social Sciences and Management ranging from printed books, e-books, back volumes and CDs\DVDs.
  • Each hostel room is well furnished.Provision stores are located in the hostel where students can buy essentials such as mattresses, pillows, buckets, mugs, locks, stationeries, books, soaps. Both AC and Non-AC rooms are available. All the hostels are wi-fi enabled. Mess facility is also available for the students. The hostels are compounded with the security detail and security cameras. The doctor is also available.
